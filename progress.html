Progress

Small improvements every week add up. Track your habits and stay consistent.

BMI (Body Mass Index)

BMI is a simple number based on height and weight. It can give a general idea of body weight, but it does not measure muscle vs. fat.

  • Underweight: below 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: 25–29.9
  • Obese: 30+

Tips that work

  • Sleep 7–9 hours
  • Drink water
  • Train with good form
  • Be consistent

Quote: “Discipline beats motivation when motivation is gone.”

Progress checklist

  • ✅ I trained (or did active recovery)
  • ✅ I ate enough protein
  • ✅ I drank enough water
  • ✅ I slept 7–9 hours
