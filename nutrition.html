Nutrition Basics

To build muscle, you need enough calories and protein every day. Keep it simple and focus on good food most of the time.

🥩 Protein foods

Helps your muscles recover and grow.

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Fish
  • Greek yogurt
  • Beans (also good)

🍚 Carbohydrates

Gives you energy for hard training.

  • Rice
  • Pasta
  • Oats
  • Potatoes
  • Fruit

🥑 Healthy fats

Supports hormones and overall health.

  • Nuts
  • Olive oil
  • Avocado
  • Peanut butter

Nutrition Basics

Simple daily meal example

Just an easy idea you can copy and adjust.

  • Breakfast: oats + milk + banana
  • Lunch: chicken + rice + vegetables
  • Snack: yogurt + nuts
  • Dinner: fish + potatoes + salad

Easy rules

  • Drink water during the day
  • Eat protein with every meal
  • Don’t skip fruits/vegetables
  • Be consistent (most important)

Tip: If you struggle to eat enough, add a healthy snack.
